What we collect

  • Account details such as your name, email address, and provider metadata.
  • Study content like notes, projects, courses, reminders, tasks, and uploads.
  • Optional integration data when you connect GitHub or Google Calendar.

How we use data

  • To run StudyOS features and sync your workspace across devices.
  • To connect optional services such as GitHub repo sync or calendar import.
  • To improve performance, reliability, and support workflows.
